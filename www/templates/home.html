The Bloomington Area Runners Association (BARA) is a collection of runners dedicated to having fun while training and racing. Located in Bloomington, home of Indiana University, the club provides organization to a once segmented running community. At BARA, our approach is simple: to provide the best possible resources and support to our members to ensure a lifetime of health and fitness. With group runs, training groups, and social events, we hope to provide sound advice and leadership that will create a strong sense of camaraderie and community.
